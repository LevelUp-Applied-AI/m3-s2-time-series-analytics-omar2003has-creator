# Executive Business Report - Amman Market Analysis



1. Revenue Trends

The business shows a strong upward trend, peaking in December 2025 at $1.87M.



Growth: Q4 2025 saw a 143.46% surge due to holiday sales.



Analysis: While January 2026 dropped by 58%, the 30-day moving average confirms the long-term baseline is higher than last year.



Efficiency: Average Order Value (AOV) hit an all-time high of $363 in March 2026, showing we are attracting higher-spending customers.



2. Customer Retention

Loyalty: Cohorts from April/May 2025 are our most loyal, with the best long-term retention.



Seasonal Risk: The massive December cohort has lower retention, likely consisting of "one-time" discount hunters.



Focus: We need a re-engagement strategy to convert holiday shoppers into repeat buyers.



3. Category Performance

Market Share: Major categories hold 30%+ share but growth is slowing down.



Shifting Mix: February and March saw a 20% growth rate in specific sub-categories, indicating a shift toward premium, higher-priced items.



Observation: We are selling lower volume but at much higher price points.



4. Recommendations

Loyalty Program: Launch a "Gold Tier" for the top 10% of spenders to protect the $363 AOV.



January Buffer: Plan a "New Year" campaign to prevent the massive post-holiday revenue slump.



Budget Shift: Reallocate marketing from stagnant categories to the "Resilient" categories identified in Q1 2026.



Targeted Retargeting: Use personalized offers for the December cohort to improve their Month-3 retention.
